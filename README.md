# Accredian
----------

Accredian is India's leading career-focused online education platform delivering 47+ executive and professional certificate programs in partnership with India's most prestigious institutions - IIM Lucknow, IIM Visakhapatnam, XLRI Jamshedpur, E&ICT Academy IIT Kanpur, SP Jain School of Global Management, IIM Tiruchirappalli, and IIM Amritsar.

Unlike traditional online program management (OPM) platforms, Accredian co-creates curriculum with its partner institutions, ensuring rigorous industry alignment. The platform specializes in AI & Generative AI, CXO Leadership (CEO, CFO, COO, CTO, CHRO, CDAIO), Data Science, Product Management, Project Management, HR Leadership, Senior Management, Business Analytics, and Strategy & Leadership.

Key Features: • 100% online, live instructor-led weekend classes • Curriculum co-created with IIM/IIT/XLRI faculty and industry experts • Full CXO program suite from IIM Visakhapatnam • 8+ dedicated AI & Generative AI programs • 1:1 career mentorship and assistance • Executive Alumni Status from partner IIMs • Capstone projects in every program • EMI options up to 24 months

Who It's For: Working professionals (3–10+ years experience), aspiring managers, senior executives, CXOs, entrepreneurs, and professionals looking to transition into AI, data science, product management, or leadership roles.

Headquarters: Gurgaon, India
